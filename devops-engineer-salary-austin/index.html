DevOps Engineer Salaries in Austin, TX

Austin's growing tech scene drives DevOps demand. Tesla and Apple infrastructure teams hiring. Remote DevOps roles from SF companies common and well-compensated.

At the median level, total compensation including bonus and benefits reaches $140k per year. The market is growing at +6.0% year-over-year.

When negotiating, anchor to the 75th percentile ($145k). Most employers build 10-15% room into initial offers. Always negotiate — it is expected.

  • Research your specific employer pay bands on Glassdoor and Levels.fyi
  • Anchor your ask to the 75th percentile ($145k) not the median
  • Negotiate total comp — signing bonus, equity, and benefits if base is capped
  • Get competing offers — multiple offers create the strongest leverage
