Software Engineer Salaries in New York

Software Engineers in New York, NY earn significantly above the national median, reflecting the city's high cost of living and intense competition for tech talent. The median base salary of $145,000 is 17% higher than the national median of $124,000 for the same role.

New York's tech sector has grown rapidly, with major employers including Google, Meta, Amazon, Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan Chase, and hundreds of fintech startups all competing for engineering talent. The financial services sector in particular pays a premium for engineers with experience in low-latency systems, distributed infrastructure, and quantitative computing.

Engineers specializing in machine learning, AI, and cloud infrastructure can expect to earn 20–35% above the median. Full-stack engineers with strong product intuition remain in high demand across NYC's growing startup ecosystem.

Negotiation Tips for NYC Software Engineers

When negotiating your Software Engineer salary in New York, always anchor your ask to the 75th percentile ($171k) rather than the median. Most employers build 10–15% negotiation room into their initial offers.

  • Use competing offers — NYC has the highest density of tech employers in the US. Multiple offers are common and create strong leverage.
  • Negotiate total comp — If base is capped, push for signing bonus, accelerated vesting, or higher RSU grants.
  • Specialize in high-demand skills — ML, AI, and cloud infrastructure command $30–50k above median in NYC.
  • Financial sector premium — Banks and hedge funds often pay 30–50% above tech company rates for the same title.
  • Remote leverage — Many NYC employers now allow hybrid. Competing remote offers from SF companies can raise your floor.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average Software Engineer salary in New York?
The median Software Engineer salary in New York, NY is $145,000 per year in 2025. Salaries range from $98,000 at the 10th percentile to $200,000 at the 90th percentile depending on experience, company size, and specialization.
How much does a senior Software Engineer make in New York?
A Senior Software Engineer in New York earns a median of $196,000 per year. Total compensation including bonus and equity typically reaches $240,000–$280,000 at FAANG and top-tier companies.
Is $145k a good salary for a Software Engineer in NYC?
$145k is exactly the median — 50% of Software Engineers in NYC earn more and 50% earn less. To be in the top 25%, you'd need $171k or above. FAANG and financial sector companies typically pay 40–60% above market median.
How does NYC Software Engineer salary compare to San Francisco?
San Francisco Software Engineers earn a median of $165,000 — about 14% more than NYC's $145,000. However, San Francisco has a significantly higher cost of living, so on a cost-adjusted basis the difference narrows considerably.
What skills increase Software Engineer salary in New York?
Machine learning and AI skills add $25–40k above median in NYC. Cloud architecture (AWS, GCP), distributed systems design, and experience with high-frequency trading infrastructure (at banks/hedge funds) are the highest-premium skills in the New York market.
