Registered Nurse Salaries in Los Angeles, CA

California nurses benefit from strict nurse-to-patient ratios mandated by state law, which drives up wages significantly. Travel nursing demand in LA remains extremely high post-pandemic, with some assignments paying $3,000–$5,000 per week.

At the median level, total compensation including bonus and benefits reaches $112k per year. The market for Registered Nurses in Los Angeles, CA is growing at +4.0% year-over-year, reflecting continued demand across the region.
