About CheckSalary.us

Free · No signup · AI powered

CheckSalary.us is a free salary benchmarking tool that helps US workers understand their market value. We believe everyone deserves access to accurate salary data — without paywalls, subscriptions, or forced signups.

How it works

Enter your job title, city, and experience level. Our AI — powered by Claude — searches real-time data from the US Bureau of Labor Statistics, Glassdoor, LinkedIn Salary, and Indeed to give you an instant, accurate salary breakdown.

You get your full percentile breakdown (10th through 90th), total compensation including bonus and equity estimates, year-over-year market growth, and specific negotiation tips for your role and city.

Our data sources

  • BLS OEWS 2024 — US Bureau of Labor Statistics Occupational Employment and Wage Statistics, covering 830 occupations across 380 metro areas
  • Glassdoor — Self-reported salary data from verified employees
  • LinkedIn Salary — Professional network salary insights
  • Indeed — Job posting and salary data from active listings

Why free?

We believe salary transparency is a public good. Workers who know their market value negotiate better, make better career decisions, and help close pay gaps. CheckSalary.us is free forever, supported by non-intrusive advertising.
